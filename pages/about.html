The Apostle Yoshua NDAGIJIMANA MASASU

Our story

“And the Lord answered me ,and said ,write the vision ,and make it plain upon tables ,that he may run that readeth it.The vision is yet for an appointed time ,but at the end it shall speak, and not lie:though it tarry ,wait for it ;because it will surely come, it will not tarry”(Habbakuk 2,2-3)

In 1984, I visited Rwanda for the first time after decades in exile and God spoke to me about the country. In the aftermath of the 1994 Genocide against Tutsi, Rwanda was a desolate country filled with blood and bones of victims who are estimated to 1 million. God commanded me to organize a mission trip to Rwanda through the earlier created ‘Jerusalem Ministry’. The mission included among others delivering a special message of God destined to the new authorities in Kigali.

On my way to Kigali God reminded me the words he had told me ten years earlier through Ezekiel 37:1-10 and confirmed that the time of great work among his people and nations starting from Rwanda had come. At that time, Rwanda was looking like the valley of dry bones Ezekiel saw in the vision which became a vast army by the power of the Word and the Spirit of the Lord.

God clearly asked us to start a church which would be a Family Church where everyone would find a father, a mother, a sister and a brother and where dried broken hearts would be restored and equipped. The ultimate vision was to bring restoration in the World where there is spiritual, emotional and physical death and dryness.

Our Startegy

The Vision would be implemented in 7 weeks( a period of seven years accomplishing a specifically task of restoration and equipping people to be restorers of others) winning souls to Christ the perfect restorer,bringing some cares then equipping then as disciples of Christ before they are sent.

Week 1: Evangelism and gathering peoples from differents back ground around Christ in the same family church

Week 2: Care Ministry of healing ,counseling ,deliverance ,restoration ,…

Week 3: Discipleship preparation (Training,equipping )

Week 4: Implementing Christ character (fruit of the Holly Spirit ) in the disciples lives

Week 5: Equipping the disciples in the gifts of the Holly Spirit for effective Ministry

Week 6: Missions/To take (Conquering) souls and Nations for Christ Jesus.

Week 7: Establishment/Building of the Kingdom of God/Take or occupy the territory for God

NB -1) those 7 Ministries/Actions/Activities are executed daily time in our Churches,we do evangelism ,we care for the needy and Christians babies,we train or raise disciples and affect or send them for the work of Ministry.But by establishing weeks(Periods of seven years each) it helps for every task/Action/Activity to become a priority/Focus/Priority of priorities for a period of 7 year.By doing this every Task will be properly executed in enough time and after such time we may seat and evaluate the achievement and check the undone before we concentrate ourselves for the next task.

2) We hold an international conference every end of a week where every leader on different level brings report on the work achieved during the week and also a proposal strategic plan for the coming week. -3) Every year we have a specific topic related to the week .By doing this it helps to focus on the same object during the year as we implement in steps the major priority of the week.
